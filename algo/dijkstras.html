Dijkstra's Algorithm

Category: Shortest Path Optimization

Description: An efficient algorithm for finding the shortest paths from a single source node to all other nodes in a weighted graph with non-negative weights.

Business Case (Amazon Logistics):

  • Optimizing delivery routes between warehouses and customer addresses.
  • Minimizing shipping cost and delivery time using real-time traffic and distance data.
  • Dynamic rerouting during disruptions or traffic congestion.
