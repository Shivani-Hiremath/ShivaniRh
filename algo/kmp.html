Knuth-Morris-Pratt (KMP) Algorithm

Category: Product Search Optimisation (E-commerce)

Description: Linear-time substring search that uses a prefix-function table to skip redundant comparisons, making “search-as-you-type” smooth on millions of product titles and descriptions.

📦 Business Case (Amazon):

  • Real-time “did you mean?” suggestions in the search bar.
  • Fast autocomplete matching against huge product catalogues.
  • Lightning search inside long product descriptions or reviews.
🧠 How It Works?
  • Pre-compute the Longest Prefix Suffix (LPS) table for the pattern (O(m)).
  • Scan the text; on mismatch, jump using LPS instead of restarting (O(n)).
  • Total complexity = O(n + m) without backtracking.
