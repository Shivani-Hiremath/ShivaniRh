Bellman-Ford Algorithm

Category: Graph Algorithms

Description: Computes shortest paths from a single source in a graph, even with negative weights.

  • Find the best delivery route even when some roads have negative conditions like tolls or delays.

  • Help in international shipping where costs between locations can vary and include taxes or fees.
  • Ensure reliable delivery planning even when some routes might have unexpected issues or slowdowns.
